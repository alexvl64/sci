Our crypto investment funds

Three complementary funds — Dynamic Trends, CryptoVision and Equinoxe — designed for different risk profiles within a regulated institutional management framework.

High (controlled) risk

Dynamic Trends

Summary

A directional strategy optimised to outperform Bitcoin.

Objective

Outperform Bitcoin over the medium / long term by capturing major bull trends while limiting corrective phases.

Offensive Block

50 % AUM

Primary block — directional Bitcoin exposure with moderate leverage (max 2x), strictly controlled.

Defensive Block

50 % AUM

Secondary block — neutral strategies, systemic arbitrage, quantitative models with controlled drawdown.

Moderate risk

CryptoVision

Summary

A defensive, disciplined strategy on major crypto-assets.

Objective

Deliver consistent performance with controlled volatility and limited drawdown, including during market instability.

Defensive Block

70 % AUM

Primary block — market-neutral strategies, systemic arbitrage, quantitative models. No directional leverage.

Offensive Block

30 % AUM

Secondary block — dynamic exposure to Bitcoin, Ethereum and liquid crypto-assets. Moderate leverage, strictly controlled.

Low risk

Services for managers

White Label Solution

SparkCore makes its regulated infrastructure available to enable third-party managers to launch their own alternative investment fund.

What SparkCore provides

  • Registered fund manager

    Registered with Finantsinspektsioon (EFSA) — supervised Small Fund Manager regime. Financial Institution licence issued by EFIU (KYC/AML).

  • Full legal structuring

    Creation of the fund vehicle (usaldusfond), drafting of regulatory documents (Partnership Agreement, Subscription Agreement, Redemption Request).

  • Operational infrastructure

    Access to platforms (Binance, Kraken, OKX, ByBit), EUR/USD banking access — SEPA & SWIFT, multi-signature security, NAV/performance management software.

  • Accounting & reporting

    Accounting handled by a Big Four firm. Production and distribution of investor reporting. Regulatory reporting to EFSA and the Bank of Estonia. Automated fee and NAV calculation.

  • Favourable taxation

    No tax at fund level. No withholding tax on distributions to foreign investors.
